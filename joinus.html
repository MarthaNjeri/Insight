    How Can I Become a Member?

    To become a member, you must:

    • Be over 18 years old
    • Own a dairy cow
    • Pay a registration fee of Ksh. 4,910

    Ready to join? Fill out our membership form.

    How Would I Benefit by Being a Member?

    • Guaranteed market for your milk produce
    • Obtaining animal feed and human food on credit
    • Availability of veterinary and Artificial Insemination services on credit
    • Linkages to financial institutions (e.g., K-Unity and Limuru Dairy SACCO) for loans
    • Free agricultural education and extension services
    • Consistent and timely payment
    • Good price for raw milk compared to other processors

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do you sell pasteurized milk?
    Yes, our milk is pasteurized and is available for sale at our factory.

    Other than selling wholesome pasteurized milk, are there by-products that you make?
    The future of our society is bright; we shall have high-value products on the shelves soon.

    What are some of the products that you sell in your Agri-hub stores?
    We have a feedmill where quality animal feed is produced. We also have stores where animal supplements, minerals, and human food like unga and cooking fat are stocked.

    Where can I get your LIMDA feed?
    Limda feed brand is available in all our stores. You can call us on 0713833814 for more information or to order.

    Do you sell feed on credit?
    Credit facility is only extended to our store members.

    Do you assist your members by giving them loans?
    Yes, we do that through our members' SACCO and K-Unity SACCO.

    Can I access Extension Services if I am not a member?
    Our extension education services are for members only.
